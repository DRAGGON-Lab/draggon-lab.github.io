This thesis is the conceptual and technical foundation for the DRAGGON Lab platform strategy. It frames synthetic biology as an engineering workflow that can be represented, automated, measured, and improved through standards-enabled data loops.

## Website role

On the lab website, this publication should anchor the DBTL workflow narrative: genetic network design, build automation, experimental characterization, data capture, and model-driven learning as one connected engineering process.
