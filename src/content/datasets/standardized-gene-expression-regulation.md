This planned dataset captures the first reusable DRAGGON Lab data product: engineered organisms and genetic constructs linked to standardized design metadata, experimental context, calibrated measurements, and model-ready representations.

## Intended contents

The dataset will include genetic designs, build and sample metadata, characterization measurements, growth and expression profiles, and links to the biological context needed to interpret each experiment.

## Intended uses

The dataset will support mechanistic parameterization, inverse-problem analysis, graph neural networks, transformer-based sequence models, and hybrid AI/ML models for gene expression and regulation.

## Quality principles

The dataset should be reusable, well-described, calibrated where possible, and explicit about biological context. The goal is to make data useful beyond the original experiment and ready for comparison across projects and models.
